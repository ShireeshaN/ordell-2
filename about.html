WHO WE ARE

Our Story

CEO & Founder

Founder & Managing Director

In 2020, Ordell Interiors was born out of a shared passion for design and innovation. Founded by Vikas Kumar and Rachana, the journey began with a vision to transform spaces with a blend of creativity and functionality.


Vikas brought his expertise in civil engineering and a unique perspective that marries structural knowledge with design aesthetics.


Rachana, with five years of experience in the software industry, channeled her passion for design into this new venture. Together, they have crafted a dynamic team that excels in both residential and commercial projects.


Starting with small-scale furniture designs, we quickly grew to handle turnkey projects, completing over 60 projects, including 32+ 2BHKs, 25+ 3BHKs, and 6+ villas. From our modest beginnings in a 500 sq. ft. office in Hyderabad, we continue to pursue excellence in every project we undertake.


Our story is about collaboration, growth, and a commitment to realizing our clients’ visions through thoughtful design and meticulous execution.
